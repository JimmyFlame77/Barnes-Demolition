What We Do

Our Demolition Services

From single-family teardowns to full commercial and industrial demolition, Barnes Demolition brings precision, safety, and power to every project in the Wilmington area.

Residential Demolition

Full and partial teardowns of homes, garages, outbuildings, and accessory structures. We handle debris removal and site cleanup — leaving your lot ready to build.

Commercial Demolition

Retail buildings, warehouses, office complexes, and mixed-use structures. Our team manages permitting, utilities coordination, and full site preparation for your next build.

Interior Demolition

Selective gut-outs, wall removal, floor stripping, and renovation teardowns — without touching your structure's shell. Perfect for remodels and tenant improvements.

Industrial Demolition

Heavy-duty dismantlement of industrial facilities, plants, silos, and large-scale structures. We utilize specialized equipment and strict safety protocols for complex projects.

Concrete & Foundation Removal

Breaking and removal of concrete slabs, driveways, foundations, and retaining walls using hydraulic breakers and excavators. We haul it away — you get a clean slate.

Site Clearing & Grading

Land clearing, tree and stump removal, soil grading, and debris hauling to prepare raw or post-demolition sites for construction. We leave your property development-ready.
